Leb Chalets

LebChalets is a website that helps users to find an ideal Chalet for their vacations inside Lebanon.
In few clicks users can search for a chalet by simply entering the desired date, location, and a budget of their choice. Finally they press the button search and let our search engine find them a suitable chalet.
Users can also search for a chalet by inserting its name.
